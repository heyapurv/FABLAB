A fabrication lab, also known as a fab lab, is a workspace equipped with tools and equipment for digital fabrication and prototyping. It is a collaborative space where people can work on creative projects, explore new technologies, and learn new skills.


In a fab lab, you can find a wide range of equipment, including 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and various hand tools. These tools allow you to create physical objects from digital designs, using materials such as wood, plastic, metal, and even fabric.
